Big or small, every organization has data. As an experienced data engineer, I can help you better manage your information and find the true value within. My volunteer services include:

Data Integration

Using best-of-breed tools and processes, I will work with your team to design, build, and deploy data extraction, transformation, and loading pipelines that you can rely on. Under a comprehensive data governance plan, we may also apply data virtualization and master data management to your critical assets.

Data Quality

Drawing on over a decade of experience in data management, I will help you identify quality problems in your data and deploy cleansing processes so your business users will fully trust your reporting.

Enterprise Search

Once your disparate data sources have been integrated and cleansed, we'll deploy a powerful Google-like search capability allowing your business users to quickly access the enterprise data they need, securely in a web browser.

Data Visualization

Gain insights into your trusted data using business intelligence tools offering rich dashboarding, interactive reporting, and timely alerts sent to your users. Custom development ensures integration with your corporate portal.

Predictive Analytics

Using the latest techniques in machine learning, I will rapidly prototype innovative analytic processes to find powerful and actionable insights in your business data. Once proven, we can promote these analytic pipelines into production.

Pay As You Go

All compute resources will be provisioned from cloud-providers using on-demand scaling to meet your business needs, now and in the future. I have experience with the major cloud providers including Amazon Web Services, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform.
