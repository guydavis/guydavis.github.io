Data Integration

Using best-of-breed tools and processes, I will work with your team to design, build, and deploy data extraction, transformation, and loading pipelines that you can rely on. Drawing on over a decade of experience in data management, I will help you identify and correct the quality problems in your data so your business will truly trust your reports and analysis.

Enterprise Search

Once your disparate data sources have been integrated and cleansed, we'll deploy a powerful Google-like search capability allowing your business users to quickly access the enterprise data they need, securely in a web browser.

Data Visualization

Gain insights into your trusted data using business intelligence tools offering rich dashboarding, interactive reporting, and scheduled reports sent to users. Custom development ensures integration with your corporate portals.

Predictive Analytics

Using the latest techniques in machine learning, I will rapidly prototype innovative analytic processes to find powerful and actionable insights in your business data. Once proven, we can promote these analytic pipelines into production.
